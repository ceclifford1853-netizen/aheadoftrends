**Approach A: "Cyber Noir Intelligence"**

- **Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist Dark Tech / Cyberpunk Minimalism
- **Core Principles:** Raw authority through contrast; data as aesthetic; machine precision meets human urgency; no decorative excess — every element earns its place
- **Color Philosophy:** Slate-950 (#020617) as the void — absolute authority. Neon Cyan (#06b6d4) as the signal cutting through darkness. Electric Blue (#3b82f6) for depth and hierarchy. Amber (#f59e0b) used sparingly as a "danger/alert" accent for scarcity and urgency cues. The palette communicates: "We operate where others can't see."
- **Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric Bento-Grid — cards of varying sizes arranged on a dark grid with subtle glowing borders. No centered hero; instead, a left-anchored command-center layout. The grid itself is visible as faint lines, like a radar screen.
- **Signature Elements:** (1) Pulsing neon glow rings on active agent nodes; (2) Monospace data readouts for metrics (growth percentages, slot counts); (3) Scanline texture overlay at 3% opacity on the hero background
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Every hover reveals hidden intelligence — trend cards flip to show share buttons, agent nodes pulse faster on hover, metric counters animate upward on scroll-entry
- **Animation:** Staggered fade-in from bottom for bento cards (60ms delay between each); typing cursor animation on hero headline; continuous slow pulse on "LIVE" indicators; number counter animations for metrics
- **Typography System:** Display: Space Grotesk Bold 700 (commanding, geometric, tech-forward); Body: DM Mono 400 for data/metrics; Prose: Inter 400 for readable paragraphs. Headlines use tracked uppercase for section labels.

- **Core Principles:** Restraint as power; whitespace as confidence; information hierarchy through scale not decoration; the interface feels like a Bloomberg terminal designed by Apple
- **Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0a0a0f) background with a barely-perceptible blue undertone. Cyan-400 (#22d3ee) as the single accent color — used only for CTAs, active states, and key metrics. All other elements in shades of slate. The restraint of the palette signals premium positioning.

- **Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, deliberate transitions (300ms ease-out). Hover states are subtle — a slight background brightening and border glow. The interface rewards careful attention.

- **Typography System:** Display: Syne ExtraBold 800 (distinctive, architectural); Body: Outfit 400/500; Monospace data: JetBrains Mono. Extreme size contrast between hero (96px) and body (16px).

## Selected Approach: **A — "Cyber Noir Intelligence"**

This approach best embodies the "Agentic Intelligence Agency" positioning — it feels like a command center for AI dominance, not a marketing website. The Bento-Grid layout directly matches the brief's specification, and the Space Grotesk + DM Mono typography pairing creates the right tension between authority and data precision. The neon cyan on slate-950 is exactly the specified color scheme.
