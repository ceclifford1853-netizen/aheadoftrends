## The Paradigm Shift

Traditional SEO optimizes for **search engine crawlers**.  
AEO (Agentic Engine Optimization) optimizes for **AI reasoning engines** like Perplexity, SearchGPT, and Claude.

## The Agentic Dominance Framework

To achieve Agentic Dominance, your content must satisfy three core layers:
1. **Machine Readability**: Structured data (Schema.org) that allows AI to parse entities.
2. **Authority Signals**: Citations from high-trust domains that validate your expertise.
3. **Conversation Fit**: Content structured as answers to complex queries, not just keyword matches.

## Implementation Steps

1. Audit your current content for EEAT signals.
2. Implement JSON-LD schema for all service pages.
3. Restructure headings to match natural language queries.
