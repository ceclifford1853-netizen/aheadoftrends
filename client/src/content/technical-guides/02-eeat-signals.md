## What is EEAT?

EEAT stands for **Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness** — the three pillars AI engines use to rank content.

### Expertise
- Author credentials and background
- Depth of knowledge demonstrated
- Technical accuracy and precision

### Authoritativeness
- Brand recognition in the industry
- Citation frequency in other authoritative sources
- Backlink profile quality

### Trustworthiness
- Transparency about sources
- Clear disclaimers and conflicts of interest
- User reviews and testimonials

## How to Build EEAT Signals

1. **Author Bio**: Include verified credentials
2. **Source Attribution**: Link to original research
3. **Expert Quotes**: Feature industry leaders
4. **Data Transparency**: Show methodology clearly
