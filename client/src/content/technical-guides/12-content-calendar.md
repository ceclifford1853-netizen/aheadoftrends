## The Power of Consistency

AI engines reward consistent, regular content updates. A content calendar ensures you stay ahead.

### Optimal Publishing Frequency

- **Minimum**: 2 guides per month
- **Recommended**: 4 guides per month
- **Aggressive**: 8+ guides per month

### Content Calendar Best Practices

1. **Plan 3 months ahead**
2. **Align with industry events**
3. **Cover gaps in competitor content**
4. **Maintain consistent quality**
