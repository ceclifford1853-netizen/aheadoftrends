## How AI Engines Choose Sources

AI engines cite content that:
- **Answers the question completely**
- **Provides verifiable facts**
- **Includes authoritative sources**
- **Is recent and relevant**

### Building Citation Momentum

1. **Create comprehensive guides** (850+ words)
2. **Include original research** (data, surveys, studies)
3. **Link to authoritative sources** (academic papers, industry reports)
4. **Optimize for AI parsing** (clear structure, schema markup)

### The Snowball Effect

Once you're cited once, AI engines are more likely to cite you again, creating a compounding advantage.
