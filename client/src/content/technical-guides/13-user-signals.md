## Critical User Signals

- **Time on Page**: How long users spend reading
- **Bounce Rate**: Percentage of users who leave immediately
- **Click-Through Rate**: How often users click from AI engine results
- **Scroll Depth**: How far users scroll through content

### Optimizing for User Engagement

1. **Clear value proposition** in the first 100 words
2. **Visual breaks** (headers, lists, images)
3. **Actionable insights** throughout
4. **Strong conclusion** that encourages sharing
