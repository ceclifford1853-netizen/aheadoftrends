## What is Schema Markup?

Schema markup is structured data that tells AI engines exactly what your content is about.

### Common Schema Types for AEO

- **Article**: News, blog posts, guides
- **Person**: Author credentials
- **Organization**: Company information
- **FAQPage**: Question-answer format
- **BreadcrumbList**: Navigation hierarchy
