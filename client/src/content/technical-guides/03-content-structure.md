## The Minimum Viable Content Length

AI engines prefer **850+ words** because it signals comprehensive coverage. Shorter content is often treated as supplementary.

### The Optimal Structure

1. **Introduction** (100 words): Problem statement + solution preview
2. **Context** (200 words): Industry background
3. **Deep Dive** (400 words): Technical details, examples, data
4. **Implementation** (150 words): Actionable steps
5. **Conclusion** (50 words): Key takeaways

### Why This Works

- **Comprehensive**: Covers all angles of the topic
- **Scannable**: Clear headers help AI parsing
- **Authoritative**: Length signals expertise
