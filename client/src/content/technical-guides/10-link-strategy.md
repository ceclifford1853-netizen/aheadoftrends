## Why Links Still Matter (But Differently)

AI engines use links as **authority signals**, but quality matters far more than quantity.

### High-Quality Link Sources

- Industry publications
- Academic institutions
- Government agencies
- Established news outlets

### Link Building Strategy

1. **Create linkable content** (original research, unique insights)
2. **Reach out to relevant publications**
3. **Participate in industry discussions**
4. **Build relationships with influencers**
