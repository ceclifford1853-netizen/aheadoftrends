## Why Traditional Keywords Don't Work for AEO

AI engines understand **semantic intent**, not keyword density. A single mention of a concept is often enough.

### AEO Keyword Principles

1. **Intent-First**: Focus on what users want to know
2. **Semantic Variations**: Use synonyms and related terms
3. **Question Format**: Answer the questions AI engines ask
4. **Long-Tail Focus**: Specific, high-intent queries
