## Brand Authority vs Content Authority

- **Content Authority**: Individual pieces rank well
- **Brand Authority**: Your entire brand is recognized as authoritative

### Building Brand Authority

1. **Consistent messaging** across all platforms
2. **Thought leadership** through original research
3. **Industry participation** (conferences, panels, publications)
4. **Community building** (engaged audience, loyal followers)

### The Long-Term Advantage

Brands with high authority receive preferential treatment from AI engines, even for new content.
