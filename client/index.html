What is AI Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AI Engine Optimization (AEO) is the discipline of structuring your website's content, metadata, and semantic markup so that AI-powered conversational engines — including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity AI, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot — can accurately discover, understand, and recommend your business to users. Unlike traditional Search Engine Optimization (SEO), which targets ranked lists of links, AEO targets the answer layer: the single, direct response an AI assistant delivers when a user asks a question.

What is Content Quality in AEO?

Content Quality measures word count, heading hierarchy from H1 through H2 and H3, image alt text coverage, and internal linking density. AI engines prefer comprehensive, well-structured content with clear topical authority. Target a minimum of 800 words per page, use a single H1 tag, and maintain at least 3 internal links per page to improve your Content Quality score.

What is Technical SEO in AEO?

Technical SEO measures meta titles between 30 and 60 characters, meta descriptions between 120 and 160 characters, HTTPS security, canonical tags, viewport configuration, and crawl accessibility. These are the technical foundations that allow AI crawlers to access and index your content reliably.

What is Authority in AEO?

Authority measures JSON-LD structured data including Organization, Article, Product, and LocalBusiness schema types, schema type richness, and outbound citations to authoritative sources. AI engines use entity recognition to determine if your business is a credible, citable source worthy of recommendation.

What is Chat Visibility in AEO?

Chat Visibility measures FAQPage schema, HowTo markup, concise answer paragraphs of 40 to 60 words, and question-based headings using What, How, and Why formats. These are the exact content formats that AI engines extract for direct conversational answers when users ask questions.

AEO vs SEO: What is the Difference?

Traditional SEO optimizes for a list of ten blue links. A user clicks one, browses, and may or may not convert. AEO optimizes for a single, authoritative recommendation delivered directly in a conversational interface. When a user asks what the best project management tool is for remote teams and an AI assistant recommends your product by name, that is a zero-click conversion event. The user already trusts the recommendation before visiting your site.

How to Improve Your AEO Score

The most impactful single improvement for most websites is implementing JSON-LD structured data. Adding an Organization schema with your business name, description, URL, logo, and contact information gives AI engines a machine-readable identity card for your business. The second highest-impact change is adding FAQPage schema to your key service pages. Third, restructure your content to include concise answer paragraphs of 40 to 60 words that directly answer common questions about your business.
