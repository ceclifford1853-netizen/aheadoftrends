What is AI Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the discipline of structuring your website's content, metadata, and semantic markup so that AI-powered conversational engines can discover and recommend your business. Unlike traditional SEO for links, AEO targets the answer layer.

Why Am I Not Showing Up on ChatGPT?

Most businesses are Google search page 3 or 4 ghosts because they lack **Entity Integrity**. If ChatGPT can’t verify who you are via machine-readable schema, it won't recommend you. Our tool finds these gaps instantly.

How Do I Get Cited by Perplexity AI?

Perplexity treats the web like a giant research paper. If your content isn't "Answer-First"—answering the user's question in the first 50 words—it skips you. We help you restructure for the "Answer Layer."

The Four Pillars of AEO Dominance

Content Quality (40%)

Word count, H1-H3 hierarchy, and topical authority. Target a minimum of 800 words per page to achieve Agentic Dominance.

Technical SEO (25%)

Meta titles, descriptions, HTTPS security, and canonical tags that allow AI crawlers to index your content reliably.

Authority (20%)

JSON-LD structured data including Organization, Article, and LocalBusiness schema types. AI uses entity recognition to determine if you are a credible source.

Chat Visibility (15%)

FAQPage schema, HowTo markup, and concise answer paragraphs of 40 to 60 words for direct conversational answers.
