What is AI Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AI Engine Optimization (AEO) is the discipline of structuring your website's content, metadata, and semantic markup so that AI-powered conversational engines — including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity AI, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot — can accurately discover, understand, and recommend your business to users.

What is Content Quality in AEO?

Content Quality measures word count, heading hierarchy from H1 through H2 and H3, image alt text coverage, and internal linking density. AI engines prefer comprehensive, well-structured content with clear topical authority.

What is Technical SEO in AEO?

Technical SEO measures meta titles, meta descriptions, HTTPS security, canonical tags, and viewport configuration. These are the technical foundations for AI crawlers.

What is Authority in AEO?

Authority measures JSON-LD structured data including Organization, Article, and Product schema types. AI engines use entity recognition to determine if your business is a credible, citable source.

What is Chat Visibility in AEO?

Chat Visibility measures FAQPage schema, HowTo markup, and concise answer paragraphs of 40 to 60 words. These are the formats AI engines extract for direct conversational answers.

AEO vs SEO: What is the Difference?

Traditional SEO optimizes for ten blue links. AEO optimizes for a single, authoritative recommendation delivered directly in a conversational interface.
