What is AI Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AI Engine Optimization (AEO) is the discipline of structuring your website's content and semantic markup so that AI-powered conversational engines — including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity AI — can accurately discover and recommend your business. Unlike traditional SEO for links, AEO targets the answer layer.

The Four Pillars of AEO Dominance

Content Quality (40% Weight)

Content Quality measures word count, heading hierarchy from H1 through H3, and internal linking density. AI engines prefer comprehensive, well-structured content with clear topical authority. Target a minimum of 800 words per page to improve your score.

Technical SEO (25% Weight)

Technical SEO measures meta titles (30-60 chars), meta descriptions (120-160 chars), HTTPS security, and canonical tags. These allow AI crawlers to access your content reliably.

Authority (20% Weight)

Authority measures JSON-LD structured data including Organization, Article, and LocalBusiness schema types. AI uses entity recognition to determine if your business is a credible source.

Chat Visibility (15% Weight)

Chat Visibility measures FAQPage schema, HowTo markup, and concise answer paragraphs of 40 to 60 words. These are the exact formats AI engines extract for direct answers.
