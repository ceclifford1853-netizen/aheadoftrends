The Evolution of Search: AEO vs SEO

Traditional SEO optimizes for a list of ten blue links. A user clicks one, browses, and decides to trust you. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) optimizes for a single, authoritative recommendation delivered directly by an AI assistant. When a user asks, "What is the best service near me?", the AI performs a real-time crawl of its training data and citations. If your business doesn't have Entity Integrity, the AI will ignore you and recommend your competitor. Our AEO diagnostic tool bridges this gap by auditing your site for machine-readability.

The Four Pillars of AI Engine Optimization

Our AEO software tool measures your visibility across four critical, evidence-based factors. First is Content Quality (40%): Word count, heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3), and internal linking density. Second is Technical SEO (25%): Metadata length, canonical tags, and mobile accessibility. Third is Authority (20%): The implementation of JSON-LD schema (FAQPage, Organization, and Software) which acts as a digital passport for AI crawlers. Fourth is Chat Visibility (15%): Using concise, answer-first paragraphs that AI engines can easily extract for direct responses.
